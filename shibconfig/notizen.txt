2) Was passiert auf der Seite des SP nachdem die session vom IDP erstellt worden ist

The browser delivers the response from the IdP to an Assertion Consumer Service endpoint at the SP.
The ACS implementation decodes the message, decrypts the assertion if necessary, and performs a variety of security checks.
If everything is in order, then the SP will create a new user session after extracting attributes and other information from the message.
Attributes are translated into a cacheable form using the SP's AttributeExtractor, passed through an AttributeFilter, and cached in the new session along with other relevant information.

ACS (Assertion consumer service)
https://wiki.shibboleth.net/confluence/display/SHIB2/NativeSPAssertionConsumerService (advanced feature - normlerweise reicht die SSO configuration)

Once the session is created, the SP determines where to send the browser by examining the "relay state" information returned by the IdP, if any.
